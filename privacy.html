Privacy Policy

At UniCart, we believe in radical transparency. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information — because your trust is the foundation of our marketplace.

📅 Effective: June 15, 2026 | Last revised: June 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

UniCart collects information to provide better services to all our users — from browsing unique products to delivering your orders. We collect the following categories of data:

CategoryExamplesPurpose
Identity & ContactName, email address, phone number, shipping/billing addressAccount creation, order fulfillment, customer support
Payment InformationCredit/debit card details, digital wallet tokens (processed via PCI-compliant partners)Payment processing & fraud prevention
Transaction DataProducts viewed, purchase history, cart activity, saved itemsPersonalized recommendations & order history
Technical DataIP address, browser type, device identifiers, cookies, log dataSite security, analytics, performance improvements

We never intentionally collect sensitive data (like precise geolocation, health info, or biometric data) without explicit consent.

2. How We Use Your Information

UniCart uses your personal data only for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Order fulfillment: Process payments, ship products, and provide order updates.
  • Personalization: Tailor product recommendations, offers, and content based on your preferences.
  • Customer support: Respond to inquiries, handle returns, and resolve disputes.
  • Security & fraud detection: Monitor suspicious activity, prevent unauthorized access, and protect against fraud.
  • Marketing & communication: With your consent, send promotional emails, unique deals, and newsletters. You can opt out anytime.
  • Legal compliance: Comply with applicable laws, regulations, and enforce our Terms of Service.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR & Global Standards)

If you reside in the European Economic Area (EEA) or other regions with specific data protection laws, we process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Contractual necessity: To fulfill orders and provide services you request.
  • Legitimate interests: To improve our platform, prevent fraud, and conduct analytics.
  • Consent: For marketing communications and optional cookies.
  • Legal obligations: To comply with tax, anti-fraud, and record-keeping laws.

4. Sharing & Disclosure of Information

UniCart values your privacy and does not sell your personal data to third parties. However, we may share information in the following limited scenarios:

  • Service providers: Trusted partners who help with payment processing, shipping, email delivery, and analytics (under strict confidentiality agreements).
  • Marketplace sellers: When you purchase from third-party sellers, we share necessary order details (name, address, product) to fulfill shipment.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, subpoena, or governmental request, or to protect the rights and safety of UniCart, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction (with notice).

🔐 Your data, your control. We never share sensitive payment information directly with sellers — all payments are tokenized and encrypted via PCI Level-1 gateways.

5. Cookies & Similar Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze trends, and personalize content. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings. Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they ensure core functionality (e.g., cart, login).

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential: Required for navigation, security, and shopping cart.
  • Analytics/Performance: Help us understand how visitors interact with UniCart (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Functional: Remember your preferences (language, currency).
  • Targeting/Advertising: Used to show relevant product recommendations and measure ad campaigns (only with consent).

By continuing to use UniCart, you consent to our use of cookies as described, except where you have configured your browser to reject them.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. For example:

  • Account information is kept while your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward for legal or tax reasons (typically 6-7 years for transaction records).
  • Marketing preferences are retained until you opt out or request deletion.
  • Cookie-related data is retained per your browser settings.

7. Your Privacy Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access & Portability: Request a copy of your personal data in a structured format.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion (Right to be forgotten): Request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Restriction & Objection: Limit how we process your data for certain purposes (e.g., direct marketing).
  • Withdraw Consent: For any processing based on consent, you may withdraw at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@unicart.com or via the methods below. We will respond within 30 days. We will never discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

8. Children's Privacy

UniCart does not knowingly sell products to minors or collect personal information from anyone under the age of 16. If we discover that a child under 16 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately. Parents or guardians who believe their child has submitted information should contact us.

9. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security safeguards to protect your information: encryption (TLS 1.3) for data in transit, AES-256 for stored sensitive data, regular vulnerability scans, and restricted access to personal data on a need-to-know basis. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. International Data Transfers

UniCart operates globally. Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States. For users in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, we ensure appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or equivalent protections.

11. Third-Party Links & Embedded Content

Our platform may contain links to external websites (e.g., brand pages, social media) or embedded content (e.g., YouTube videos). These third parties have their own privacy policies, and UniCart is not responsible for their practices. We encourage you to review their policies before interacting.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. The "Effective Date" at the top indicates when the latest version was posted. If material changes occur, we will notify you via email or a prominent notice on our website before the changes become effective.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data handling practices, please reach out to our Data Protection team:

  • Email: privacy@unicart.com
  • Mail: UniCart Privacy Office, 123 Commerce Lane, Suite 400, Wilmington, DE 19801, USA
  • For EU residents: You may also lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority (DPA).

💚 UniCart Promise: We treat your data like our own — with respect, integrity, and protection. No shady selling, no hidden trackers.
